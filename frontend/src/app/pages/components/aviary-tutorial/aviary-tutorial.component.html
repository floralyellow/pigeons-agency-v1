Here you can find your pigeons. Pigeons generate droppings over time.
You can sell your pigeons to gain feathers (needed to level up)

Assign up to 5 pigeon per team (A or B), that will be used to attack other players (pvp), or to launch adventures (pve).
You can also select which team you want to use for defenses from other players.

Pigeons have 3 main stats during fights: strength :fight: magic & shield.
Strength pigeons usually have the highest base values, but strength is diminished by shields.
Magic pigeons are usually a bit lower than pure strength, but magic is not impacted by shields..
Shield pigeons have shield (and a bit of magic).. The shield value is multiplied by the number of opposing strength pigeons.
Formula : Total strength value +Total magic value - ( opposing shield * number of my strength pigeons)
