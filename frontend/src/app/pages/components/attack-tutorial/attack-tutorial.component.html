You can attack other players to try and climb up the leaderboard. You can even steal some of their droppings if you win fights.

If there is too much of a difference in military score between you and the opponent, you won't win anything.

You can only attack players once every 2 minutes max. If a player is attacked, he is protected for a period of 20 minutes. (Unless he attacks someone in the meantime !)

Beware of attacking higher level players, their pigeons might be way stronger !
